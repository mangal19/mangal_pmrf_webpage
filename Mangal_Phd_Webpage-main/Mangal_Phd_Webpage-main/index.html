About
I am a second-year PhD research scholar in the prestigious 'Department of Chemical Engineering' at IIT Guwahati, under the esteemed mentorship of Professor "Tamal Banerjee". My research focus lies on synthesizing and characterizing bioplastic materials, particularly from non-edible oils like castor oil and linseed oil. These bioplastics offer eco-friendly solutions for diverse applications, including sustainable packaging and films.
